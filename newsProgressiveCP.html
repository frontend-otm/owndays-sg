WHAT ARE
PROGRESSIVE LENSES?

Progressive lenses provide seamless, all-distance vision correction (far, intermediate, near), making them ideal for individuals developing presbyopia around age 40

UNDERSTANDING PRESBYOPIA

Presbyopia is a natural condition starting around age 40 where the eye's lens hardens, making close-up vision blurry. While irreversible, this loss of near focus is easily corrected with reading glasses, contact lenses, or surgery.
