WHY OWNDAYS CONTACT LENS?

UV Protection
Our lenses feature an advanced UV-filtering layer that shields your corneas from the sun's most harmful rays (~95% UV-B and 80% UV-A).
Hyaluronic Acid
Each lens is saturated with hyaluronic acid (HA), which helps lock in moisture and reduces friction every time you blink.
High Water Content
A higher water percentage allows more oxygen to reach the eye, boosting oxygen permeability (Dk/t) of 30, for clearer, whiter eyes all day.
Ultra-Thin Design
Our precision-cut, ultra-thin profile (0.08 mm centre thickness) mimics the natural curvature of the cornea.
Homegrown Excellence
Designed and manufactured in Singapore under strict quality control standards for superior consistency.
Shape Retention
A resilient hydrogel matrix lets the lens hold its bowl shape on your fingertip; no more flipping inside-out.
Anti Blue Light
An integrated high-energy-visible (HEV) filter blocks up to 15% of blue light in the 400–450 nm range, helping reduce digital eye strain.
